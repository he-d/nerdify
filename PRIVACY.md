# Nerdify — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 3 July 2026_

**Nerdify does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal or usage
data.** It runs entirely on your own device.

## What Nerdify does

Nerdify applies a bundled [Nerd Font](https://www.nerdfonts.com/) to the web
pages you choose to enable it on. That is its only function.

## Data collection

None. Specifically, Nerdify:

- makes **no network requests** of any kind;
- contains **no analytics, tracking, or telemetry**;
- does **not read, collect, or transmit** the content of the pages you visit;
- does **not collect** any personal information.

## Preferences storage

Your settings — selected font, scope (all text / code only), the global on/off
toggle, and per-site overrides — are saved using the browser's built-in
`storage.sync` API. This data stays within your own browser profile. If you have
browser profile sync enabled, the browser may sync it across your own signed-in
devices; it is never sent to the developer or to any third party.

## Permissions and why they are used

- **storage** — save your preferences (font, scope, per-site overrides).
- **activeTab / tabs** — read the active tab's hostname so a "this site"
  override applies to the correct domain. No browsing history is collected.
- **Host access (`<all_urls>`)** — apply the chosen font to whatever site you
  enable Nerdify on. Page content is never read, collected, or transmitted.
